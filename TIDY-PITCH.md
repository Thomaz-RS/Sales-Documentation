## 🎭 Tonality & Delivery

### DO:
✅ **Be consultative** - Ask questions, listen first  
✅ **Use their specific numbers** - Not generic examples  
✅ **Show confidence** - You know the product works  
✅ **Make it conversational** - Not scripted  
✅ **Let pauses breathe** - Don't rush the math  

### DON'T:
❌ **Be pushy** - Let the data speak  
❌ **Use jargon** - Talk like a human  
❌ **Trash competitors** - Position TIDY as better, not them as bad  
❌ **Promise features that don't exist** - Be honest  
❌ **Ramble** - Be concise
